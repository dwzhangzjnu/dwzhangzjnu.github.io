We are currently looking for two or four self-motivated master students each year to work on interesting research in computer vision, such as object detection, visual tracking, image super-resolution, fine-grained visual recognition, person re-identification, lightweight neural networks, adversarial attack and other related areas. Qualified students will be supported by research assistantships.

We prefer the students who have solid mathematical foundations, strong programming skills, and good English abilities (listening/reading/writing). The experiences in research (e.g. projects, academic competition, publications, etc.) will be a benefit. If you are highly creative, and interested in computer vision, please do not hesitate to send your CV and transcripts of grades to davidzhang@zjnu.edu.cn

Admissions Commitmentss:

  • Provide a full range of effective academic guidance in algorithm design, experimental verification, paper writing, patent application, etc, so that the students are expected to gain solid professional knowledge, strong practical ability, broad academic vision.

  • Provide you enjoyable scientific research atmosphere, comfortable office environment, generous academic allowance/rewards, and efficient gpu computing equipment.

  • For the students who succesfully get their reseach papers accepted by top conferences, we will financially support them to participate in the conferences. Extra bonus is also distributed.

This should be enough to get you started research. Happy coding!
