Short Bio

Dawei Zhang is currently an Assistant Professor with the Department of Computer Science and Technology at the Zhejiang Normal University. Before that, he received his Ph.D. degree (advised by Professor Zhonglong Zheng and Xiaoqin Zhang) in 2022. During 2021, he worked with Prof. Yanwei Fu as an intern at Institute of Science and Technology for Brain-inspired Intelligence (ISTBI), Fudan University. His research interests include computer vision, deep learning, and multimedia computing.
