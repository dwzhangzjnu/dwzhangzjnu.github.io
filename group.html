The Visual Intelligence and Multimedia Systems (VIMS) group is a part of the Machine Learning and Vision Laboratory in the School of Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang Normal University. The VIMS group is led by Dr. Dawei Zhang.

Call for Students

Research Topics

  • Computer Vision: Object Detection, Motion and Tracking, Saliency Detection, Person Re-ID, Medical Image Segmentation, Multi-modal Fusion, Crowd Counting and Localization, Low-light Image Enhancement, Vision & Language, etc.

  • Deep Learning: Convolutional Neural Networks, Attention Mechanism, Vision Transformer, Light-weight Neural Network, Weakly-supervised Learning, Prompt Learning, Reinforcement Learning, Adversarial Attack, Domain Adaption, etc.
